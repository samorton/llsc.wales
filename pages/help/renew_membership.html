How to renew my club membership

Follow these steps to renew your club membership

  1. Go to the Members' Portal.
  2. Enter your username and password in the respective fields.
  3. Click the "Log In" button.

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Follow these steps to log in to your account:

  1. Go to the Members' Portal.
  2. Enter your username and password in the respective fields.
  3. Click the "Log In" button.
  4. You will be taken to your personal profile. You can change personal details here, including photo if you like.
  5. Click the "Membership" link
  6. You should see the membership options that are appropriate for you. Click the one you’re interested in.
  7. Add requested additional data, and add any appropriate boat -park space, where you have a boat or kayak space allocated to you. You will be able to pay for more than one space
  8. Choose the way you’d prefer to pay. One-oQ or recurring subscription by bank card
  9. Click Finish, this will either complain you’re missing data or prompt you to "check-out"
  10. Click "Proceed to Cart"
  11. You will see a summary of your order.
  12. Offline payment is Cash – this will be reconciled over the bar. Pay with Card is what we would prefer.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at llansailing@gmail.com.
