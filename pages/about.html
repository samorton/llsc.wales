About Us

If you like your seaside with a peaceful, period twist you’ll love Llanfairfechan. It’s a small Victorian resort that – in common with Llandudno across the bay – remains true to its roots. There’s a prom fronting a wide stretch of low-tide sands plus a range of retro facilities that include a boating lake and bowling green.
And, as well as those traditional time-honoured ‘beside the sea’ pleasures Llanfairfechan is also popular with windsurfers and kitesurfers.
